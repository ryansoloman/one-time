Crafting the seat of comfort since 1998.

Our Story

Richfield Chairs began with a simple observation: the world is full of chairs, but very few truly understand the human form. What started in a small workshop in Richfield has grown into a global pursuit of ergonomic perfection.

We don't just manufacture furniture; we engineer experiences. Every curve, every stitch, and every material choice is made with the intention of providing lasting support for those who dream, work, and rest in our seats.

Why Choose Richfield?

Premium Materials

From sustainably sourced hardwoods to top-grain Italian leathers, we never compromise on the raw elements.

Ergonomic Science

Our designs are tested against rigorous orthopedic standards to ensure your spine is always in the right place.

Timeless Design

We avoid "fast furniture" trends. A Richfield chair is designed to look as modern in twenty years as it does today.

Commitment to Earth

We believe that luxury shouldn't cost the planet. Richfield is committed to a carbon-neutral manufacturing process by 2030. We use recycled alloys and ensure that for every tree used in our frames, two more are planted in its place.
