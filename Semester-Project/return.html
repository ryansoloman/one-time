Return & Refund Policy

Last updated: January 30, 2026

We want you to love your purchase! If you’re not 100% satisfied, we’re here to help. You have 30 calendar days to return an item from the date you received it.

Condition Note: To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused, in the same condition that you received it, and in the original packaging.

Refund Process Timeline

Stage Action Timeframe
Return Shipping Package arrives at our warehouse 3-5 Business Days
Inspection Quality check of returned item 48 Hours
Refund Issue Credit sent to original payment method 5-10 Business Days

Non-Returnable Items

Some items are exempt from being returned:

  • Gift cards
  • Downloadable software products
  • Personal care items (e.g., used cosmetics)
  • Final Sale items

How to Start a Return

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Visit our Online Returns Portal with your order number.
  2. Print the prepaid shipping label provided.
  3. Drop off the package at any authorized carrier location.
