1. Shipping & Delivery

We specialize in getting your seating to you safely. Please note the following:

  • Processing: In-stock chairs ship within 48 hours.
  • Delivery Types: We offer Standard Curbside Delivery and White Glove Assembly.
  • Weight Limits: Shipping costs are calculated based on the total weight of the order at checkout.

Important: Inspection upon Arrival

Furniture is bulky and prone to transit friction. You must inspect the box for visible damage before signing the delivery receipt. If the box is damaged, please write "Damaged upon receipt" on the delivery slip.

We provide a 1-year limited warranty on frames, springs, and pneumatic lifts. This does not cover "pilling" of fabrics or natural variations in wood grain or leather scarring.
