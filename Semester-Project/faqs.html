Common Inquiries

Standard delivery within the continental PK typically arrives in 3-5 business days. Bespoke orders from the LEO or AURA collections may require up to 10 days for precision handling and white-glove setup.

To ensure the structural integrity of our artisan components, chairs are shipped in reinforced modules. Our patented "Quick-Click" system allows for a complete, tool-free assembly in under 15 minutes.

We invite you to experience our seating in your own environment. If the ergonomic profile does not perfectly align with your needs within 30 days, we offer a seamless return process with complimentary pickup.

Every ChairCo product is backed by a 2-year limited warranty. This covers all precision mechanisms, including the gas lift, recline tensioners, and 4D adjustable armrests.
